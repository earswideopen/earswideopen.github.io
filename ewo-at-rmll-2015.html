Ears Wide Open at the RMLL 2015

Matthieu Berjon Sunday, July 12, 2015 | Translations: fr

The project Ears Wide Open (ewo) has been presented at the Rencontres Mondiales du Logiciel Libre (RMLL) in Beauvais (France). I had the chance to introduce the project with Marie-Pierre Bijon, a company works doctor, concerned by the hearing impairements and their consequences on a company.

You can:

  • watch the recording of the conference on the platform of the RMLL (Transcript of the video),
  • download the slides of Marie-Pierre's presentation,
  • download my presention (about the hearing aids suggestions by itself)
